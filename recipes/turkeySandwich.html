Turkey Sandwich

Description

A turkey sandwich is a great lunch option that will leave you satisfied!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Turkey
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Grab two slices of bread and toast them
  2. Add mayo to inside part of each slice
  3. Add turkey meat to bread
  4. Layer lettuce, tomato, and onion
  5. Add bread slice on top to close the sandwich
  6. Cut in half and enjoy!
